Refunds & Cancellation

Effective date: May 30, 2026

This policy applies to the paid Counselor Tier subscription of the Great Physician app, operated by The Kiln Ministries. Payments and billing are handled securely by Stripe.

Free trial

Every subscription begins with a 14-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged at all.

Cancelling your subscription

  • You can cancel at any time, from within the app or by contacting us.
  • Cancellation stops all future renewals.
  • After cancelling, you keep access to the Counselor Tier until the end of the billing period you have already paid for.

Refunds

  • First charge — 14-day money-back guarantee. If you are charged at the end of your trial and are not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of that first charge for a full refund.
  • Renewals. Renewal payments are generally non-refundable, and we do not provide prorated refunds for the unused portion of a billing period. If you were charged unexpectedly (for example, you intended to cancel before a renewal), contact us and we will review your request in good faith.
  • Statutory rights. Nothing in this policy limits any non-waivable refund or withdrawal rights you may have under the consumer-protection laws of your country or state.

How to request a refund or cancel

Email hello@thegreatphysician.ai with the email address on your account and a brief note. Refunds, when granted, are issued to the original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.
