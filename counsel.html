The Counselor Tier

The Great Physician app is free for anyone seeking biblical counsel. The Counselor Tier is a paid professional subscription that gives practicing biblical counselors a set of tools to document, draft, and track their counseling work.

What's included

Whetstone

Paste a session transcript and get an AI-assisted draft of an ACBC-style case report, field by field, that you review, edit, and finalize.

Research Assistant

Ask theological and case questions across a curated library of the nouthetic and biblical-counseling tradition.

Spiritual Growth Tracking

Score each session across three axes — Christ's sufficiency, love of neighbor, and God's glory — and chart a counselee's growth over time.

Counselee & Session Records

Keep counselee profiles, session notes, and assign homework — organized in one place, privacy-minded by design.
